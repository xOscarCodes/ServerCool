We are a team of developers who have created an IoT-based project that helps in temperature automation in server rooms. Our aim is to provide a reliable and efficient solution to the problem of overheating in server rooms, which can cause serious damage to equipment and disrupt business operations. Our project uses sensors to monitor the temperature in server rooms and automatically adjust the cooling systems to maintain optimal conditions.
